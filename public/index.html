Leaders Of Digital

Season: Artificial Intelligence

Is being implemented within the framework of the federal project «Artificial Intelligence» of the national program «Digital Economy of the Russian Federation».

  • The project is aimed at finding, developing and supporting talented specialists in creating products and services using artificial intelligence technologies.
  • Within the framework of the hackathons of the project, development specialists jointly solve the business problem and (or) social problem posed by the organizers in the field of artificial intelligence for a limited time.
  • According to the results of the competition, prize-winners and winners are determined.
